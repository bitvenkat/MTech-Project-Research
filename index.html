Edge AI for UAVs: Research Synthesis

I. Architectural Foundations: Edge AI Constraints

The primary challenge in deploying advanced Machine Learning on Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) using platforms like the Raspberry Pi is the **severe trade-off between power consumption and real-time inference speed**. Autonomous drone feasibility depends on minimizing total end-to-end latency to **sub-50 ms** for critical dynamic tasks like collision avoidance, while simultaneously extending battery life. This section introduces the core computational bottlenecks that high-impact research must address.
